## What is CanvasUI?

CanvasUI Stream Manager is a complete overlay solution that includes:

- **Visual Scene Editor** — Design your stream layouts by dragging and positioning modules
- **Built-in Web Server** — Serves overlays directly to OBS, no separate setup needed
- **Live Reload** — Changes in the editor instantly update in OBS
- **Multi-Scene Support** — Different layouts for Gaming, Just Chatting, BRB, etc.
- **OBS Integration** — Automatically switches layouts when OBS changes scenes (via Streamer.bot)
- **Media Library** — Upload and manage images/videos for your overlays
- **Module System** — Chat, emotes, audio visualiser, webcam, images, videos, and more
